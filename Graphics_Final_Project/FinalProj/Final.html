Draw: Click the button of the shape you want to draw, then click the "define" button. To draw all shapes other than polygon and curve, click and drag on the canvas. To draw polygon, click where you want each point, a path will be drawn between each click; to stop drawing the polygon double click to create your last path. To draw a curve, click the three points where you want the curve.
Select: Select the shape you want to transform or copy: click the "select" button then click the desired shape in the canvas.
Transform: Once a shape is selected, click the button with the corresponding action you wish to perform (Translate, Scale, Rotate). Click and drag on the canvas to see the effect.
Save file: To save your current canvas to a JSON file, click the "save" button.
Load file:To load in a saved JSON file, click "choose file". Once you have selected the local file, click the "load" button to see it in your canvas.
